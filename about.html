Khushi Patel

Visual‑first UI/UX designer who cares about how products feel, not just how they look.

I enjoy reading between the lines of briefs, research, and behavior to understand what people actually need from a product—then translating that into simple, confident interfaces.

My process usually starts with flows and journeys, then moves into structure, typography, and motion details. I love using visuals to communicate states and feedback clearly so users never feel lost.

Recently I’ve been exploring food ordering, retail journeys, and service touchpoints. I like adding small moments of personality—like floating sunflowers—while keeping the experience clean and accessible.

Skills & tools

  • UI layout systems, visual hierarchy, typography
  • User flows, journey maps, and interaction states
  • Figma, basic prototyping, and design handoff
  • Designing for both clarity and personality
