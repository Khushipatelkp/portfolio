Khushi Patel

Visual‑first UI/UX designer who cares about how products feel, not just how they look.

My work sits at the intersection of visual design and interaction design. I enjoy turning messy problem statements into simple, calm interfaces that help people focus on what matters.

I’m comfortable moving from research notes and flows into detailed layout decisions, typography, and motion. Figma is my main playground, but I also care about how design hands off to development.

Recently, I’ve explored food ordering, retail journeys, and service touchpoints. I’m excited by projects where I can combine systems thinking with playful visuals—like adding subtle sunflowers while keeping the experience clean and accessible.

Skills & tools

  • UI design, layout systems, and typography
  • User flows, journey maps, and interaction states
  • Figma, basic prototyping, and design handoff
  • Brand‑aware visual exploration and component thinking
